VISION

The vision of Nalanda International School is to be of WORLD CLASS in school learning and innovation driven by social sensitivity and latest teaching methodology. Therefore, with the help of experts, we will be among one of the best schools in qualitative education and inputs for personality development to suit ever-changing needs of Industry and society.

PRINCIPAL MESSAGE

At Nalanda International School, we are committed to making learning an enjoyable, motivating and inspiring experience with the right blend of academics, co-curricular and sports activities. We provide an atmosphere which is conducive to today’s contemporary world and help build ‘global honchos’. Our efforts are supported by a team of highly competent, dedicated and efficient staff, who facilitate students to learn research, probe and explore. The ultra-modern infrastructure with facilities like well-equipped laboratories, Maths lab, English Lab, swimming pool (big and small), dedicated montessori (indoor) play ground, sprawling sports ground, skating rink, boxing arena, tennis court, gymnastics hall, shooting range and a spacious auditorium along with serene ambience provides the right environment for the multi- faceted growth of our children.
